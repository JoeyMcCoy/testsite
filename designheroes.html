BOOK FORMS

Design Heroes

Project completed in a Book Forms course for our top three design heroes. We were to come up with a page layout that would be consistent for all three artists and then translate that over so it is capable of being used on an iPad. On the iPad versions, the images of the artist and their work would be able to scroll as well as the text to the right of it.
